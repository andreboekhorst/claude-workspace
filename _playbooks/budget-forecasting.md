# Budget Forecasting

A workspace for getting control of budgets and forecasts. Covers tracking actual spend against plan, building forward-looking models with multiple scenarios, and producing reports that help people make informed financial decisions.

## Core problems this workspace solves

- Spending is not tracked consistently, so no one has a reliable picture of where money is going until it's too late.
- Forecasts are disconnected from actuals — projections are made once and never updated as real numbers come in.
- Reporting is manual and time-consuming, pulling data from multiple sources into formats that still aren't clear.
- There is no scenario planning, so when conditions change, the team has no pre-built models to evaluate options.
- Budget discussions lack structure, leading to decisions driven by who argues loudest rather than by data.

## Action blueprints to use

- **Research** — Gather and organize historical spending data, existing budgets, and financial baselines into a clear picture.
- **Compare** — Build side-by-side scenario models (best case, expected, worst case) to evaluate different budget paths.
- **Summarize** — Distill complex financial data into concise reports and dashboards for stakeholders and leadership.
- **Review** — Set up regular budget review cycles to compare forecasts against actuals and flag variances early.
- **Document** — Record budget assumptions, methodology, and decisions so future forecasting builds on past work.

## Required tools & skills

- No external tools required. This playbook works with file-based analysis and documentation.
- Useful if available: spreadsheet tools for quantitative modeling and data manipulation.

## Useful references

- Historical budgets and year-over-year spending data
- Financial reports (monthly, quarterly, annual)
- Department-level spending breakdowns and cost center mappings
- Revenue projections and business plans that inform budget assumptions
- Accounting categories and chart of accounts for consistent classification

## Folder structure

```
files/
  budgets/       # Current and historical budget documents, allocations, and actuals
  forecasts/     # Scenario models, projections, and variance analyses
  reports/       # Formatted reports, summaries, and presentation-ready materials
```
