# Podcast Production

**Follow-up:** What's your podcast about, and what format do you use?

## Skills

### Plan Content
Episode calendar with topics and guests.
1. Clarify show themes, cadence, and audience
2. Generate a batch of episode ideas with angles
3. Sequence into a release calendar
4. Note guest candidates and booking priorities

In: show concept, past episodes | Out: content calendar → `files/episodes/`

### Research
Guest background and topic prep.
1. Identify what to research — guest, topic, or both
2. Gather bio, past interviews, key positions
3. Surface interesting angles and potential tensions
4. Compile a one-page research brief

In: guest name, topic | Out: research brief → `files/guests/`

### Draft
Episode scripts and show notes.
1. Review research brief and episode angle
2. Write intro, segment structure, and outro
3. Draft show notes with timestamps and links
4. Flag sponsorship and CTA placements

In: research brief, episode plan | Out: script, show notes → `files/scripts/`

### Prep
Interview question prep.
1. Review guest research and episode angle
2. Draft questions ordered by arc — warm-up, core, close
3. Add follow-up prompts for likely responses
4. Include one wild-card question

In: research brief, episode plan | Out: question sheet → `files/scripts/`

### Review
Post-episode retrospective.
1. Summarize what worked and what didn't
2. Note standout moments and clips worth repurposing
3. Capture listener feedback themes
4. List improvements for next episode

In: episode notes, listener feedback | Out: retrospective → `files/episodes/`

## Tools
- **Google Calendar** — schedule recordings, manage release dates

## Folders
- `files/episodes/` — episode plans, calendars, retrospectives
- `files/guests/` — research briefs, contact notes
- `files/scripts/` — scripts, show notes, question sheets
