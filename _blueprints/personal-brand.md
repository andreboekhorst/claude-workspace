# Personal Brand

**Follow-up:** What field are you in, and which platforms do you use?

## Skills

### Research
Audience analysis, competitive landscape, and platform trends.
1. Define your target audience and niche
2. Map competitors and peers — what works for them
3. Identify platform-specific trends and formats
4. Summarize positioning opportunities

In: current profiles, screenshots of analytics | Out: research summary → `files/analytics/`

### Plan Content
Build a content calendar across platforms.
1. Define themes and content pillars
2. Map topics to a weekly or monthly cadence
3. Assign formats — post, article, thread, video
4. Set publishing dates and review points

In: brand positioning, content themes | Out: content calendar → `files/content/`

### Draft
Write bios, posts, articles, or about pages.
1. Clarify audience, platform, and purpose
2. Match tone to brand voice
3. Draft full piece
4. Edit for clarity, punch, and platform fit

In: topic, brand guidelines, profile images | Out: written draft → `files/content/`

### Brainstorm
Generate positioning angles, content themes, and collaboration ideas.
1. Explore what makes you distinctive
2. List content angles and series concepts
3. Identify collaboration and guest opportunities
4. Select ideas with the most leverage

In: brand goals, audience research | Out: idea list → `files/content/`

### Review
Periodic brand audit — consistency, growth, engagement.
1. Review content performance across platforms
2. Assess brand consistency — voice, visuals, messaging
3. Flag gaps or drift from positioning
4. Recommend adjustments for next period

In: screenshots of analytics, recent content | Out: audit report → `files/analytics/`

## Tools
- **WebSearch** — research trends, competitors, platform best practices

## Folders
- `files/content/` — drafts, calendars, published pieces
- `files/assets/` — bios, headshots, brand guidelines
- `files/analytics/` — audience research, performance reviews, audits
