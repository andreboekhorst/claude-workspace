# YouTube Channel

**Follow-up:** What's your channel about, and who's your audience?

## Skills

### Plan Content
Video calendar with topics, formats, and hooks.
1. Clarify niche, audience, and upload cadence
2. Generate video ideas with working titles and angles
3. Sequence into a release calendar with variety
4. Tag each with format — tutorial, essay, vlog, list, etc.

In: channel concept, past videos, analytics screenshots | Out: content calendar → `files/scripts/`

### Research
Topic research, competitor analysis, and trending searches.
1. Define the video topic and target search terms
2. Research what's already ranking and what's missing
3. Gather facts, stats, and examples to reference
4. Compile a research brief with unique angle

In: topic, competitor links | Out: research brief → `files/scripts/`

### Draft
Write scripts with hooks, structure, and CTAs.
1. Review research brief and video format
2. Write a hook — first 30 seconds that earns the click
3. Structure the body with clear sections and transitions
4. Close with CTA and end-screen strategy

In: research brief, video plan | Out: script → `files/scripts/`

### Brainstorm
Thumbnail concepts and title variations.
1. Review the video topic and target emotion
2. Generate 5+ title variations — curiosity, urgency, clarity
3. Describe thumbnail concepts — composition, text, expression
4. Pair strongest title-thumbnail combos

In: video topic, reference thumbnail images | Out: title and thumbnail concepts → `files/thumbnails/`

### Review
Post-publish analytics review and content retrospective.
1. Review performance data — CTR, retention, views, comments
2. Identify what worked and what underperformed
3. Compare against similar past videos
4. List actionable takeaways for future content

In: analytics screenshots, CSVs, audience comments | Out: retrospective → `files/analytics/`

## Tools
- **WebSearch** — research trends, competitor analysis, SEO

## Folders
- `files/scripts/` — video scripts, research briefs, content calendars
- `files/thumbnails/` — thumbnail concepts and reference images
- `files/analytics/` — performance reviews, retrospectives
