# Meeting Operations

**Follow-up:** What kind of meetings do you run most?

## Skills

### Prep
Prepare agendas, talking points, and background material.
1. Clarify meeting purpose and attendees
2. Draft time-boxed agenda items by priority
3. Prepare talking points per item
4. Gather background material and open items

In: meeting context, prior notes | Out: meeting agenda → `files/agendas/`

### Debrief
Process meeting notes into decisions, actions, and open items.
1. Read raw notes in full
2. Extract decisions and action items with owners
3. Identify open questions
4. Format into structured debrief

In: raw notes, attendees | Out: structured notes → `files/notes/`

### Checklist
Build reusable pre- and post-meeting checklists.
1. Define the meeting process to standardize
2. Break into discrete steps
3. Add quality checks at critical points
4. Group into logical sections

In: meeting process | Out: meeting checklist → `files/actions/`

### Summarize
Condense meeting content into a stakeholder-ready summary.
1. Read the full notes
2. Extract key points and decisions
3. Structure into a scannable format
4. Calibrate to target length

In: meeting notes | Out: concise summary → `files/notes/`

### Decide
Frame a choice, weigh trade-offs, and document the decision.
1. Restate the decision as a clear question
2. List options including "do nothing"
3. Score against key criteria
4. Document recommendation with trade-offs

In: decision context, options | Out: decision record → `files/notes/`

## Tools
- **Google Calendar** — view upcoming meetings and scheduling conflicts

## Folders
- `files/agendas/` — pre-meeting agendas and pre-read materials
- `files/notes/` — raw and processed meeting notes
- `files/actions/` — action item lists and follow-up tracking
