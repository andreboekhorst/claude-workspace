# Personal Finance

**Follow-up:** What is your main financial goal?

## Skills

### Research
Explore investment options, tax strategies, and financial products.
1. Define the question or knowledge gap
2. Gather relevant information and options
3. Summarize findings with pros and cons
4. Flag risks and assumptions

In: financial question, CSV bank exports, screenshots of accounts | Out: research summary → `files/reports/`

### Plan
Build a financial roadmap with goals, timeline, and milestones.
1. List financial goals and prioritize
2. Map each goal to a timeline
3. Define milestones and checkpoints
4. Identify income, savings, and investment levers

In: goals, current financial snapshot, spreadsheets | Out: financial plan → `files/goals/`

### Compare
Evaluate financial products or strategies side-by-side.
1. List options with key attributes
2. Score against criteria — returns, risk, fees, liquidity
3. Highlight trade-offs and hidden costs
4. Recommend with reasoning

In: product details, rate sheets | Out: comparison table → `files/reports/`

### Review
Monthly or quarterly financial check-in.
1. Compare actuals to budget
2. Track progress toward goals
3. Flag overspending or missed targets
4. Adjust plan for next period

In: bank exports, budget, previous review | Out: review notes → `files/reports/`

### Summarize
Net worth snapshot and progress report.
1. Tally assets and liabilities
2. Calculate net worth and delta from last snapshot
3. Highlight key movements
4. Format as a concise one-page summary

In: account balances, previous snapshot | Out: progress report → `files/reports/`

## Tools
None.

## Folders
- `files/budgets/` — monthly budgets, spending breakdowns
- `files/goals/` — financial plans, savings targets
- `files/reports/` — reviews, snapshots, research summaries
