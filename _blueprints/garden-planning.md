# Garden Planning

**Follow-up:** What kind of garden are you planning, and what's your climate zone?

## Skills

### Research
Plant compatibility, climate zones, and soil needs.
1. Clarify zone, sun exposure, and soil type
2. Research which plants thrive in these conditions
3. Check companion planting and spacing requirements
4. Compile a plant reference sheet with care notes

In: climate zone, garden photos, soil test results | Out: plant reference → `files/resources/`

### Plan
Seasonal planting calendar with layout.
1. Review plant reference and available space
2. Map planting dates — start indoors, transplant, direct sow
3. Design a rough bed layout with spacing
4. Output a month-by-month calendar

In: plant reference, garden dimensions, photos of the space | Out: planting calendar → `files/plans/`

### Checklist
Planting tasks and maintenance schedules.
1. Pull tasks from the planting calendar for the current period
2. Add recurring maintenance — watering, feeding, pruning
3. Include pest and disease watch items
4. Output as a checkable weekly task list

In: planting calendar, current date | Out: task checklist → `files/plans/`

### Draft
Garden journal entries and observations.
1. Record what was planted, transplanted, or harvested
2. Note weather, pest activity, and growth observations
3. Attach or reference photos of progress or issues
4. Flag anything to research or adjust

In: observations, garden photos, images of plant issues | Out: journal entry → `files/journal/`

### Review
End-of-season harvest review and next-year planning.
1. Summarize what grew well and what struggled
2. Review yields against expectations
3. Note lessons — timing, varieties, techniques
4. Draft initial plans and seed orders for next season

In: journal entries, harvest notes, photos | Out: season review → `files/journal/`

## Tools
None required.

## Folders
- `files/plans/` — planting calendars, bed layouts, task checklists
- `files/journal/` — garden journal entries, season reviews
- `files/resources/` — plant references, soil notes, seed catalogs
