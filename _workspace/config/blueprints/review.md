# Review

Help the user look back on a defined period, measure progress against goals, and set clear priorities for the period ahead.

## Typical flow

1. **Review** accomplishments — list what was completed or progressed during the period.
2. **Check** against goals — compare accomplishments to planned objectives or priorities.
3. **Identify** blockers — surface what slowed progress or remains stuck.
4. **Celebrate** wins — acknowledge what went well, even small things.
5. **Set** priorities for next period — choose 3-5 focus areas going forward.
6. **Compile** the review — assemble into a summary document.

## Inputs

- The period under review (e.g., this week, last month, Q1).
- Goals or priorities that were set for that period.
- Calendar data, task lists, or notes from the period.
- Any metrics or outcomes worth tracking.

## Outputs

- A review summary containing:
  - Period covered.
  - Accomplishments and progress highlights.
  - Goal status (on track, behind, completed, dropped).
  - Blockers and what to do about them.
  - Priorities for the next period (ranked).

## Quality signals

- Accomplishments are factual, not inflated.
- Goal check is honest — behind is acknowledged, not hidden.
- Blockers include at least one proposed action.
- Next-period priorities are few enough to actually focus on (3-5 max).
- The review is comparable to previous reviews for trend spotting.

## Common variations

- **Weekly review**: Light and fast, 15-minute cadence, task-level granularity.
- **Monthly review**: Medium depth, includes metrics and habit tracking.
- **Quarterly review**: Strategic, connects to annual goals and big-picture themes.
- **Team review**: Aggregates individual progress, focuses on shared goals and coordination.
