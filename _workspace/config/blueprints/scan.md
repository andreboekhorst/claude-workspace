# Scan

## Typical flow
1. **Define domain** — Clarify the space, industry, or topic to scan
2. **Cast wide net** — Search broadly across news, publications, discussions, and releases
3. **Cluster signals** — Group findings into signal categories (emerging, growing, fading)
4. **Highlight trends** — Identify the strongest patterns and notable outliers
5. **Deliver report** — Present a trend summary with signal strength ratings

## Inputs
- A domain, industry, or topic area to scan
- Optional: time window, geographic focus, specific angles of interest

## Outputs
- Trend report organized by signal clusters
- Signal strength rating: Strong / Moderate / Weak / Noise
- Notable outliers or surprises worth watching
- Sources for each signal

## Quality signals
- Breadth matters — covers multiple angles, not just the obvious headlines
- Signals are categorized by strength, not just listed
- Distinguishes between established trends and early signals
- Includes contrarian or unexpected findings, not just consensus

## Common variations
- Industry scan (what's happening in a specific market)
- Competitive landscape scan (what competitors or peers are doing)
- Technology scan (emerging tools, frameworks, approaches)
- Periodic scan (recurring scan to track how signals evolve over time)
